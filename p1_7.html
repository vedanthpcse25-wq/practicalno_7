About

Rabindranath Tagore was a great Indian poet, writer and philosopher. He won the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1913. He wrote India's national anthem "Jana Gana Mana".

Achievements

  • First Asian Nobel Prize winner
  • Author of Gitanjali
  • Founded Visva-Bharati University
  • Great poet and philosopher
